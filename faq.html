FAQs

1. What is an autorickshaw?
An autorickshaw, also known as a tuk-tuk, is a three-wheeled vehicle used for public transportation. It's a compact and maneuverable option for navigating city streets.

2. How do I book an autorickshaw?
You can book an autorickshaw through our website or mobile app. Simply enter your pickup location, destination, and preferred time, and we’ll confirm your booking.

3. Are your drivers licensed and trained?
Yes, all our drivers are licensed professionals who undergo training to ensure safe and courteous service.

4. What are your operating hours?
We operate [insert your operating hours], ensuring you have access to transportation whenever you need it.

5. How is the fare calculated?
Fares are calculated based on distance and time taken. You can view the estimated fare during the booking process.

6. Do you offer rides for groups?
Yes, we can accommodate small groups in our larger autorickshaw models. For bigger groups, please contact us for special arrangements.

7. What safety measures do you have in place?
We prioritize passenger safety by ensuring all vehicles are regularly maintained, drivers follow safety protocols, and we provide GPS tracking for every ride.

8. Can I request a specific driver?
While we strive to provide consistent service, we cannot guarantee a specific driver for each ride. However, we do our best to match you with your preferred driver whenever possible.

9. What should I do if I left something in the autorickshaw?
If you’ve left an item behind, please contact our customer support team immediately with your trip details, and we’ll assist you in recovering your belongings.

10. Do you offer any discounts or promotions?
Yes, we frequently offer discounts and promotions. Keep an eye on our website and social media pages for the latest deals!

If you have any other questions or need assistance, feel free to reach out to our customer support team!
